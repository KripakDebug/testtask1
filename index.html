Discover the Best Sports Bars in Czech Republic

Whether you're a local or just visiting, find the perfect spot to enjoy your favorite games!

Dubliner Irish Bar

Location: Tyn 1, Old Town, Prague 1

The Dubliner Irish Bar in Prague has everything a good Irish pub should: lively music, great atmosphere and fine beers. This warm, welcoming pub is also a sports bar, with 2 large screens and 14 TV's showing Premiership and International football, plus American NFL football. Ideal for the big match, and Prague stag nights and hen parties on a pub crawl.

Join the Excitement!

Rocky O'Reillys Irish Bar

Location: Stepanska 32, New Town, Prague 1

Rocky O’Reillys offers a taste of Ireland in the centre of Prague, with friendly Irish hospitality, and Guinness and Kilkenny on tap. A projection screen and TVs show live sports, the sounds of which are often drowned out by the lively tunes. The bar is popular with groups, evident by the fact that some stag parties don't seem to leave for the whole weekend!

Score Big!

McCarthy's Irish Pub & Disco

Location: Rytirska 10, Old Town, Prague 1

Well situated between the Old Town Square and Wenceslas Square, McCarthy's has the aged Irish pub feel off to a tee. It offers an alluring mix of satisfying drinks, lively atmosphere and big screen coverage of top sports. But for good measure it also houses a disco in the basement. Attracts everyone from couples and expats to stag do and hen party groups.

Join the Action!

James Joyce Irish Pub

Location: U Obecniho dvora 4, Old Town, Prague 1

A little piece of Ireland in a quiet corner of Prague, just five minutes walk from the Old Town Square. James Joyce is a traditional Irish pub with a casual atmosphere, offering a warm and hearty welcome, and some of the freshest Guinness in Prague. There is TV coverage of major sports occasions, in a calmer setting than other sports bars in Prague.

Experience Excellence!

Caffrey's Irish Bar

Location: Old Town Square 10, Old Town, Prague 1

Caffrey’s is a modern Irish bar on the Old Town Square. The clientele is mostly international, there to chat or watch live sports. In the warmer months, Caffrey's opens a terrace on the square, which attracts stag parties, hen parties and other tourists wandering by. Drinks are expensive compared to bars off the beaten track.
